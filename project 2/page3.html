Morning Routine

7:00 am - 8:00 am

Shower

After I wake up, the first thing I do to wake me up is take a shower to get me ready for the long day ahead of me.

Picking a Look

After the shower, I pick out two different outfits for the day, one for my internship, and one after. This includes picking out an outfit, accessories, and shoes. This is usually pretty important because I work in fashion.

Hair & Makeup

This step is usually very quick, I do my normal makeup routine and blow dry my hair as fast as humanly possible. To finish the look, I always spray on my perfume.

Tidy Up Bedroom

If there is enough time, I usually try to make my bed, because I won't be back until 11pm in my room. If there isn't enough time, which there usually isn't, I just let it be.

8:00 am - 8:30 am

Iced Coffee

The most important part of my morning routine, is the iced coffee. Over the years I have perfected my recipe and it's top secret. Sorry.

Breakfast

I usually try to have a good breakfast, but more often than not there isn't enough time. However, I love having berries in the morning!
